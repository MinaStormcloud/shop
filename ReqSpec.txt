Product requirements and product specifications for Westside Belle Cakery

Product requirements/User stories:
As a customer, I want to be able to....

1. Order a cake with a theme that suits a particular occasion
2. Have it customized with personal decorations
3. Have the cake delivered


Product specifications:
The product shall....

1. Look delicious
2. Have 1 or more layers based on sponge cake or chocolate cake
3. Have icing and decorations in accordance with a theme (such as chocolate, fruit or marzipan)
4. Have a price, a description, and an image on the web site
